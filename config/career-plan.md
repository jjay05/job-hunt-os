## SUCCESS CRITERIA AND RED FLAGS

Good fit:
- AI is core, not marketing
- Metrics ownership is clear and sits with product
- Startup mindset: autonomy, speed, experimentation
- Experienced AI practitioners on the team to learn from
- Clear 2-year learning trajectory
- Comp 200k+ base plus equity

Red flags:
- AI is a thin wrapper, not building with foundation models
- PM is an order-taker; metrics live in a data science black box
- Bureaucratic, consensus-driven, slow
- Comp materially below 200k base (unless dream-tier)
- No clear learning path

---

## SCREENING QUESTIONS I ASK THEM

- How does this PM role own metrics versus data science?
- Who is responsible for inference cost?
- What happens when model performance regresses?
- What is the 2-year learning path for this role?
- How is AI changing the expectations of this role?

If they cannot answer the metrics questions clearly, it is a data science black box. Avoid.
