## HANDLING THE "ADTECH PERSON" OBJECTION

Do not say: "I am pivoting from adtech to AI." It sounds like starting over.
Do say: "Adtech was my domain expertise. AI is where my builder skills go next. I have already shipped four AI systems and I build agentic projects on my own."

Reframe domain as range, not limitation: marketplace dynamics, supply-demand matching, trust and fraud, data-heavy products, automation, and operational tooling are exactly the muscles applied AI roles need. I am domain-agnostic; the systems thinking transfers.

---

## TAILORING NOTES FOR AI COMPANIES

Lead with the AI systems and personal projects, not the adtech P&L. Translate adtech wins into AI-relevant language: marketplace matching, decision support, human-in-the-loop guardrails, evals, automation ROI. Surface metrics fluency: I want to own inference cost, model quality, safety signals, and adoption, not just revenue.
